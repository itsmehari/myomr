## 🔍 **How to Find What You Need**

### Looking for Schools?

1. Go to Main Menu → Explore Places
2. Click "Schools"
3. Use search bar to find specific school
4. Browse through results
5. Click on school for details

### Looking for a Job?

1. Click "Post Job Vacancy in OMR" button on homepage
2. Or go to Services → Job Search
3. Browse current opportunities
4. Post your own job listing

### Want to List Your Business?

1. Click "Advertise Your Business in OMR" on homepage
2. Fill out the listing form
3. Submit for review
4. Your business appears in directory

### Need Healthcare?

1. Go to Explore Places → Hospitals
2. Search by location or name
3. Find contact details
4. Call directly or visit
