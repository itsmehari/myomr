## 🎯 Executive Summary

### Purpose

This document identifies **100+ problem statements** faced by job seekers and employers in OMR Chennai, which will inform:
- Landing page messaging
- Marketing content
- Value propositions
- Feature development priorities
- Competitive positioning

### Key Insights

**Job Seekers Face:**
- Long commute to city jobs
- Limited local job opportunities
- Lack of response from employers
- Difficulty finding OMR-specific jobs
- High competition in city job portals

**Employers Face:**

- Difficulty finding local candidates
- Too many irrelevant applications

## 👤 Job Seeker Problem Statements (50+)

### Category 1: Geographic & Location Issues (10 problems)

1. **Long commute to city jobs** - Spending 2-3 hours daily traveling to Chennai city for work, affecting work-life balance and increasing transportation costs.

2. **Limited local job opportunities** - Difficulty finding jobs in OMR area, forcing job seekers to look in city centers far from home.

3. **No OMR-specific job portal** - Major job portals (Naukri, Indeed) show jobs across entire Chennai, making it hard to find OMR-specific opportunities.

4. **Unclear job locations** - Job postings don't specify exact location in OMR, leading to confusion about commute distance.

5. **Jobs listed as "Chennai" but actually in OMR** - Job seekers can't filter by OMR area, missing relevant opportunities.

6. **Lack of location-based job alerts** - No way to get notified of jobs specifically in OMR area (Perungudi, Sholinganallur, etc.).

7. **No nearby job search feature** - Can't search for jobs within specific distance from home/work in OMR.

8. **Difficulty finding part-time jobs in OMR** - Limited options for students and part-time workers looking for flexible hours locally.

9. **No work-from-home OMR jobs listed** - Remote jobs not clearly marked as OMR-based, causing confusion.

10. **Transportation dependency** - Jobs in OMR require reliable transportation, but listings don't mention accessibility by public transport.

---

### Category 2: Job Search & Discovery Issues (10 problems)

11. **Too many irrelevant job listings** - Job portals show thousands of jobs, most of which don't match qualifications or location preferences.

12. **Outdated job listings** - Many job postings are expired or already filled, but still showing in search results.

13. **No response from employers** - Applying to multiple jobs but rarely getting responses, even automated acknowledgment emails.

14. **Difficulty finding entry-level jobs** - Most job portals prioritize experienced candidates, making it hard for freshers to find opportunities.

15. **Limited job categories** - Major portals don't have specific categories for OMR industries (IT parks, schools, hospitals, retail).

16. **No job recommendations** - Job portals don't suggest relevant jobs based on profile, skills, or previous applications.

17. **Poor search functionality** - Search filters don't work well, returning irrelevant results or missing relevant jobs.

18. **No saved searches** - Can't save search criteria to quickly check for new jobs matching preferences.

19. **Limited job alerts customization** - Job alerts are too generic, sending too many irrelevant notifications.

20. **No job market insights** - Don't know which industries are hiring in OMR, what skills are in demand, or salary ranges.

---

### Category 3: Application & Communication Issues (10 problems)

21. **Complex application process** - Many job portals require lengthy forms, multiple documents, and time-consuming application steps.

22. **No direct employer contact** - Can't contact employers directly through job portals, only through application forms.

23. **Application status unknown** - After applying, no way to track application status or know if employer has viewed the application.

24. **Duplicate applications** - Accidentally applying to same job multiple times or not knowing if already applied.

25. **Resume format issues** - Job portals have strict resume format requirements, causing application rejections.

26. **No cover letter templates** - Have to write cover letters from scratch for each application, time-consuming process.

27. **Missing application confirmation** - No email or notification confirming application was successfully submitted.

28. **No interview scheduling** - Can't schedule interviews directly through job portal, requiring separate email/phone coordination.

29. **Language barriers** - Some job portals and employers use English only, making it difficult for non-English speakers.

30. **No feedback on rejected applications** - Employers don't provide feedback on why applications were rejected, making improvement difficult.

---

### Category 4: Profile & Resume Issues (8 problems)

31. **Resume not ATS-friendly** - Resumes not optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), causing automatic rejections.

32. **No resume builder** - Have to create resume externally and upload, no built-in resume creation tool.

33. **Profile not visible to employers** - Job portals don't allow employers to discover candidates proactively, only through applications.

34. **Difficulty updating profile** - Profile update process is complicated, requiring multiple steps and verification.

35. **No skills assessment** - Can't demonstrate skills through tests or assessments, only through resume.

36. **Limited profile customization** - Can't highlight unique skills, projects, or achievements effectively.

37. **No portfolio integration** - Can't link portfolio, GitHub, or other work samples to profile.

38. **Profile privacy concerns** - Worried about current employer seeing job search activity on profile.

---

### Category 5: Cost & Access Issues (7 problems)

39. **Premium features required** - Many job portals require paid subscriptions to apply to premium jobs or see salary information.

40. **Hidden fees** - Some job portals charge for resume downloads, profile views, or application submissions.

41. **Limited free features** - Free accounts have severe limitations, forcing job seekers to upgrade.

42. **No free job alerts** - Premium job alert features require payment, limiting access to job notifications.

43. **Expensive resume services** - Resume writing and optimization services are expensive, not affordable for many job seekers.

44. **No free career counseling** - Career guidance and job search advice require paid consultations.

45. **Premium job listings** - Best job opportunities are hidden behind paywalls, accessible only to premium members.

---

### Category 6: Experience & Skill Development Issues (5 problems)

46. **No skill development resources** - Job portals don't offer courses, certifications, or learning resources to improve skills.

47. **Limited career guidance** - No advice on career paths, salary expectations, or industry trends in OMR.

48. **No interview preparation help** - Have to prepare for interviews independently, no mock interviews or practice resources.

49. **Difficulty showcasing projects** - Can't effectively showcase projects, internships, or freelance work on job portals.

50. **No networking opportunities** - Job portals don't facilitate networking with professionals or employers in OMR area.

---

### Category 7: Trust & Credibility Issues (5 problems)

51. **Fake job postings** - Many job portals have fake or scam job listings, wasting time and risking personal information.

52. **No employer verification** - Can't verify if employers are legitimate or if job postings are real.

53. **Privacy concerns** - Worried about personal information being shared or sold to third parties.

54. **No job posting reviews** - Can't see reviews or ratings of employers or job postings from other candidates.

55. **Unclear job requirements** - Job descriptions are vague or misleading, leading to mismatched expectations.

## 🏢 Employer Problem Statements (50+)

### Category 1: Cost & Pricing Issues (10 problems)

1. **High cost of job postings** - Major job portals (Naukri, Indeed) charge ₹5000-15000 per job posting, expensive for small businesses.

2. **No free job posting options** - All major job portals require payment, even for basic job listings.

3. **Premium features required** - Must pay extra for featured listings, resume access, or priority placement.

4. **Hidden fees** - Additional charges for resume downloads, candidate contact, or extended posting duration.

5. **Expensive bulk packages** - Buying multiple job postings requires expensive annual packages, not affordable for small businesses.

6. **No pay-per-application model** - Forced to pay upfront regardless of application quality or quantity.

7. **Recurring subscription fees** - Must maintain expensive subscriptions even when not actively hiring.

8. **No local pricing options** - National job portals don't offer region-specific pricing for OMR area.

9. **Expensive resume database access** - Accessing candidate resume database costs ₹50000-100000+ per year.

10. **No trial or free tier** - Can't test job portal before committing to expensive paid plan.

---

### Category 2: Candidate Quality & Relevance Issues (10 problems)

11. **Too many irrelevant applications** - Receiving hundreds of applications from candidates who don't match job requirements.

12. **Difficulty finding local candidates** - Major job portals show candidates from entire Chennai or India, hard to find OMR-based candidates.

13. **No candidate filtering** - Can't filter candidates by location, experience level, or specific skills effectively.

14. **Low-quality applications** - Many applications are incomplete, have spelling errors, or don't follow application instructions.

15. **Candidates not reading job description** - Applicants apply without reading requirements, wasting time reviewing irrelevant applications.

16. **No pre-screening tools** - Can't assess candidates' skills or qualifications before interview.

17. **Difficulty finding freshers** - Hard to find entry-level candidates or recent graduates for fresher positions.

18. **No candidate verification** - Can't verify candidate credentials, experience, or education claims.

19. **Spam applications** - Receiving fake or automated applications from bots or scammers.

20. **No candidate shortlisting tools** - Must manually review all applications, no AI-powered shortlisting features.

---

### Category 3: Job Posting & Management Issues (10 problems)

21. **Complex job posting process** - Posting jobs requires multiple steps, forms, and verification processes.

22. **Limited job description formatting** - Can't format job descriptions effectively, limited to plain text or basic HTML.

23. **No job template library** - Have to write job descriptions from scratch for each posting.

24. **Difficulty updating job postings** - Updating job details or closing positions requires complex processes.

25. **No job posting analytics** - Can't see how many views, applications, or candidate engagement each job posting receives.

26. **Limited job posting duration** - Job postings expire after 30-60 days, requiring reposting and additional fees.

27. **No job posting scheduling** - Can't schedule job postings to go live at specific dates or times.

28. **No multi-location support** - Can't post same job for multiple locations (Perungudi, Sholinganallur, etc.) efficiently.

29. **No job posting templates** - Must create job descriptions from scratch, time-consuming for multiple positions.

30. **Limited job categories** - Job portals don't have specific categories for OMR industries or local business types.

---

### Category 4: Application Management Issues (8 problems)

31. **No application tracking system** - Can't track application status, candidate progress, or interview scheduling in one place.

32. **Difficulty managing multiple applications** - Receiving many applications but no organized system to review and respond.

33. **No automated responses** - Can't send automated acknowledgment emails or rejection letters to candidates.

34. **Limited communication tools** - Can't communicate with candidates directly through job portal, requiring external email/phone.

35. **No interview scheduling** - Can't schedule interviews directly through job portal, requiring separate coordination.

36. **Difficulty exporting applications** - Can't export application data or resumes for offline review or sharing with team.

37. **No application notes** - Can't add notes or comments to applications for internal team review.

38. **No candidate comparison tools** - Can't compare multiple candidates side-by-side or rank applications.

### Category 5: Branding & Visibility Issues (7 problems)

39. **Generic job portal branding** - Job postings look the same as competitors, no way to showcase company brand.

40. **Limited company profile customization** - Can't create detailed company profiles with photos, videos, or culture information.

41. **No featured employer options** - Can't get featured placement or priority visibility for job postings.

42. **Difficulty standing out** - Job postings get lost among thousands of other listings, hard to get noticed.

43. **No employer reviews** - Candidates can't see company reviews or ratings, affecting employer brand.

44. **Limited social media integration** - Can't easily share job postings on social media or company website.

45. **No employer branding tools** - Can't showcase company culture, benefits, or employee testimonials effectively.

### Category 6: Local Market & OMR-Specific Issues (5 problems)

46. **No OMR-specific job portal** - Major job portals don't have dedicated OMR area focus, missing local talent pool.

47. **Difficulty finding OMR-based candidates** - Can't filter candidates by OMR location or nearby areas.

48. **No local job fair or event integration** - Can't connect with local job fairs, career events, or OMR community activities.

49. **Limited OMR industry knowledge** - Job portals don't understand OMR-specific industries, IT parks, or local business ecosystem.

50. **No local partnership opportunities** - Can't partner with OMR colleges, training institutes, or local organizations for recruitment.

## 🚀 Implementation Roadmap — Revision 2.0 (November 2025)

> Second major revision capturing the execution-focused work breakdown for modernising the MyOMR.in Job Portal across user, employer, and admin experiences.

### 1. Discovery & Planning
- Stakeholder interviews (product, ops, marketing)
- Map current user, employer, and admin workflows end-to-end
- Baseline analytics and performance (traffic, conversion, server load)
- Consolidate requirements and define success metrics

### 2. Data & Backend Stabilisation
- Normalise enums (status, job type) and clean legacy records
- Refactor listings query to use parameterised SQL filters with database pagination
- Review indexing and schema across `job_postings`, `job_applications`, `employers`
- Introduce reusable filter/query helper layer and document the API surface

### 3. Search & Listings Experience
- Redesign filter UI (chips, job-type fix, salary sliders, reset state)
- Enhance search (autocomplete, facets, saved searches, alerts)
- Update results grid (loading skeletons, consistent cards, informative empty states)
- Tune performance (defer non-critical assets, audit CSS/JS payloads)

### 4. Job Detail & Application Flow
- Polish detail layout (clear hierarchy, related jobs, sharing hooks)
- Expand application form (resume upload, structured fields, validation logic)
- Improve confirmations & notifications (email, status page, follow-ups)
- Execute accessibility & UX QA (keyboard flows, ARIA, responsive coverage)

### 5. Employer Portal Enhancements
- Strengthen authentication (magic link/OTP, verification states, session hardening)
- Build multi-step job-post wizard with drafts and preview
- Refresh employer dashboard (metrics, filters, job status management)
- Automate notification workflows (application alerts, status change updates)

### 6. Admin Dashboard & Operations
- Redesign dashboard widgets (workload, SLA tracking, approval queues)
- Implement moderation tooling (filters, bulk actions, audit trails, notes)
- Enhance employer verification pipeline (evidence capture, risk flags)
- Deliver reporting & export features (CSV, scheduled summaries)

### 7. Search Infrastructure & Scalability
- Evaluate full-text vs external search service requirements
- Implement appropriate search indices/tagging and caching strategy
- Configure monitoring & alerting for search latency/errors

### 8. Quality Assurance & Launch
- Draft comprehensive test plan (feature, regression, load, accessibility)
- Deploy to staging and run UAT with key stakeholders
- Coordinate production rollout (migrations, comms, support readiness)
- Monitor post-launch metrics and triage follow-up backlog

### 9. Documentation & Enablement
- Update product/technical docs, runbooks, and onboarding material
- Train admin/support teams on new workflows
- Publish knowledge base content for employers and job seekers
