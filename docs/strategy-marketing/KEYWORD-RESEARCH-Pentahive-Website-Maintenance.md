# 🔍 Keyword Research: Website Maintenance vs Design & Development

**Date:** January 2025  
**Purpose:** Determine optimal keywords for Pentahive Website Maintenance Service landing pages

---

## 📊 Search Volume Comparison

### Primary Keywords

| Keyword | Monthly Search Volume | Competition | Notes |
|---------|----------------------|-------------|-------|
| **Website Maintenance** | ~260 (low) | Low | Very specific, low traffic |
| **Website Maintenance Services** | ~260 | Low | Slightly better than single term |
| **Web Design** | 368,000 - 823,000 | Very High | Highest volume, very competitive |
| **Website Design** | 368,000 | High | High volume, competitive |
| **Web Development** | 135,000 - 301,000 | High | Good volume, competitive |
| **Website Design and Development** | 14,800 | Medium | Combined term, better balance |
| **Web Development Services** | Higher | Medium-High | Good volume for services |

### Key Findings

1. **"Website Maintenance" has LOW search volume** (~260 monthly searches)
   - ✅ Very specific intent (high conversion potential)
   - ❌ Limited traffic potential
   - ✅ Low competition (easier to rank)

2. **"Website Design" and "Web Development" have HIGH search volume**
   - ✅ 368,000+ monthly searches
   - ❌ Very high competition
   - ❌ May not match service offering (maintenance-focused)

3. **Combined terms offer better balance**
   - "Website Design and Development": 14,800 searches, medium competition
   - Includes maintenance as part of broader service
