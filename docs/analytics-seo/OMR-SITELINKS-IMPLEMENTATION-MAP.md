- Branded: `myomr`, `my omr`, `myomr chennai`
- Non-branded core: `omr road`, `omr`

  - `omr road events`

## Internal Linking Rules

- Keep hub labels stable in both header and footer.
- Use root-absolute paths in nav/footer (`/path`) to avoid directory-relative drift.
- Every major hub should link to the other major hubs using natural OMR Road intent copy.
- Keep canonical URLs aligned with sitemap URLs exactly.
