PRE-PUBLISH GATE (MyOMR job research CSV)
========================================

File: research-aggregator-vacancies-2026.csv

Column: db_ready_gate
  yes = verified_yes_no is "yes" AND blockers field is empty (no text in blockers).
  no  = everything else (partial data, aggregator conflicts, or explicit blockers).

Column: pre_publish_checklist
  Action items to complete BEFORE inserting into employers / job_postings on live DB.
  Even when db_ready_gate=yes, still: paraphrase descriptions, confirm listing open, fill employer contact policy fields.

File: research-aggregator-vacancies-db-ready.csv
  Subset of rows that passed db_ready_gate at research time (IDs 8 and 11).
  Not an instruction to insert automatically—human review and consent still required.
